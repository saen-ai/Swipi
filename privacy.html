Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Swipi ("Swipe Photo Cleaner"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

Swipi is designed to help you manage and clean your photo gallery efficiently. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your data stays secure.

Information We Access

Photo Library Access

Swipi requires access to your device's photo library to display, organize, and help you manage your photos and videos. All photo processing happens entirely on your device.

Storage Information

We access basic storage information to show you how much space photos and videos are using on your device.

Device Information

We may collect basic device information (device model, OS version) to improve app performance and fix bugs.

Data Storage & Security

Local Processing Only

All your photos and data are processed locally on your device. We do NOT upload, store, or transmit your photos to any external server.

No Cloud Storage

Swipi does not use cloud storage for your media files. Your photos never leave your device.

App Preferences

Settings like dark mode, notification preferences, and app configuration are stored locally on your device using secure storage.

What We Don't Collect

  • Your photos or videos
  • Your personal contacts
  • Your location data
  • Your browsing history
  • Any personally identifiable information
  • Your photo metadata (EXIF data is read locally only)

Photo Deletion

When you swipe to delete photos in Swipi, the photos are moved to your device's "Recently Deleted" folder (where supported). This gives you a recovery window before permanent deletion.

Swipi does not permanently delete photos without your explicit confirmation. You always have the final say.

Children's Privacy

Swipi is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Your Rights

  • Revoke photo library access at any time via device Settings
  • Delete the app and all locally stored preferences
  • Request information about what data we process
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
