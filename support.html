Frequently Asked Questions

How does Swipi work?

Swipi shows your photos one by one. Swipe right to keep a photo, swipe left to mark it for deletion. Once you're done, review and confirm which photos to delete.

Will my photos be permanently deleted?

When you confirm deletion, photos are moved to your device's "Recently Deleted" folder. You have 30 days to recover them before they're permanently removed.

Does Swipi upload my photos anywhere?

No. All processing happens locally on your device. Your photos never leave your phone. We don't upload, store, or transmit any of your media.

How do I recover a photo I accidentally deleted?

Go to your device's Photos app → Albums → Recently Deleted. You can restore any photo within 30 days of deletion.

What is the Random Selection feature?

Random Selection picks 15 photos from across your entire library so you can quickly review and clean up forgotten photos. It refreshes every 24 hours.

How do I find duplicate photos?

Tap "Duplicates" in the Utilities section on the home screen. Swipi scans your library and groups similar-looking photos together so you can keep the best one.

Why does Swipi need photo access?

Swipi needs access to your photo library to display, organize, and help you manage your photos. Without this permission, the app cannot function.
